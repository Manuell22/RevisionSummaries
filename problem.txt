There are 5 adjacent rooms 1 , 2 , 3 , 4 , 5. 
At the start of each turn you choose which room to search.
There is a smart ghost, which chooses which room to hide in. 
It chooses after it has seen which room you are about to search.
Initially the ghost can pick any room it wants on turn 1.
On every turn after that, the ghost can only pick a room adjacent to the one it occupied the previous turn.
The ghost must change rooms every turn, it cannot stay in the same room two turns in a row.
The ghost is smart, so it will always try to avoid being found.
If in room 1 it can only move to 2, if in room 5 it can only move to 4.
You win if you search the room the ghost is hiding in.
What is the minimum number of turns required to guarantee that you will find the ghost, regardless of how it moves?
What is the strategy to achieve this?
Are there multiple strategies that can guarantee finding the ghost in the minimum number of turns?
